Who am I?


My name is Eric David Mariasis and I recently graduated from Tufts University with a Bachelor of Science

in Computer Science from the Engineering School. In July of 2014, I began a position as a software engineer

at Raytheon doing C++ programming for radar-based technologies. When I am not hard at work, I am either

reading a fascinating book, exercising, or socializing with friends in the city.
